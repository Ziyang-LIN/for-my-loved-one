Location: Siteless
Date: 2022
Type: Individual Project
Supervised by: Faye Song

PROJECT BRIEF

Nestled within a community neighbourhood, this experiential children’s bookstore stands as an emblem of the timeless essence of books. This unique space is designed around the intriguing question: “What makes ‘a book’ a book?”. It champions the ethos of ‘learning by experiencing’, aligning perfectly with children’s innate curiosity, and promoting exploration.

In communities valuing education and shared experiences, this bookstore becomes invaluable. It provides more than just children’s literature; it offers a space for collective growth, exploration, and bonding. Such neighbourhoods, filled with young families and educators, will find the bookstore a central hub for nurturing intellectual development.

The relationship between the community and the bookstore is symbiotic. While the bookstore seeds a love for reading and discovery, the community reciprocates with unwavering support and a constant influx of inquisitive young minds. This mutual relationship ensures the bookstore’s relevance and continued evolution, making it a vital asset to the community it serves.

In essence, the Experiential Children’s Bookstore is both a tribute to the power of books and a foundational pillar for communities prioritizing learning and togetherness.
